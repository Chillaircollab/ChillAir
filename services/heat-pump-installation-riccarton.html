Heat Pump Installation in Riccarton

From character villas off Riccarton Road to rentals near the university and the cafes around Church Corner, the right heat pump keeps the place warm in winter and cool through summer. We install Daikin, Mitsubishi and Fujitsu systems for Riccarton homes, rental properties and businesses, backed by a 5-year workmanship warranty.

Heat Pumps for Riccarton Homes, Rentals & Businesses

Riccarton is one of central Christchurch's busiest and most varied suburbs, a mix of older character villas and bungalows, newer townhouses and flats, the student-heavy streets near the University of Canterbury, and the retail and hospitality strip along Riccarton Road through to Church Corner. The older housing in particular, with its high ceilings and single glazing, is exactly the sort of home that gets a big comfort and power-bill win from a modern heat pump replacing old electric or fixed heating.

Chill Air covers the whole of Christchurch, so Riccarton is right in our patch. Owner Greg is a certified refrigeration engineer who'll size the system to your room and place it for even, quiet heat, whether it's the living room of a family villa, a tidy unit you rent out, or the front of a Riccarton Road shop.

Landlords: Healthy Homes Heating, Sorted

Riccarton has a lot of rental housing, and the Healthy Homes standards require a fixed heater that can warm the main living room to 18°C. A correctly sized heat pump is the simplest way to meet that standard and keep tenants comfortable. We'll assess the living area, recommend the right capacity, and give you the paperwork you need.

  • Heating sized to meet the Healthy Homes living-room standard
  • Quick, tidy installs that suit tenanted properties
  • Reliable brands that keep running cost and call-backs down
  • Advice for single rentals or a whole portfolio
