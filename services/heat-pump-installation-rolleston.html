Heat Pump Installation in Rolleston

Rolleston is one of the fastest-growing towns in New Zealand, and a new home deserves the right heating from day one. We install Daikin, Mitsubishi and Fujitsu heat pumps for Rolleston families, backed by a 5-year workmanship warranty.

Christchurch-wide and Canterbury-wide. Rolleston is just one of the many areas we look after. Chill Air covers all of Christchurch and the wider Canterbury region, from Rangiora and Kaiapoi in the north to Rolleston, Lincoln, Ashburton and beyond. Wherever you are, we'll come to you.

Heat Pumps for Rolleston's Growing Community

Rolleston has boomed over the last decade, with new estates and family homes going up across the town faster than just about anywhere else in the country. For a brand-new Rolleston build, a well-chosen heat pump is usually top of the list, it's the most efficient way to heat the open-plan living areas these homes are designed around, and to keep things cool when the Canterbury summer kicks in.

Chill Air has fitted heat pumps in homes right across Rolleston, and we know the new-build layouts well. Owner Greg is a certified refrigeration engineer who'll size the system to your floor plan and place it for even, quiet heat, so your first winter in the new place is a warm one.
