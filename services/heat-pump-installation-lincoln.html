Heat Pumps Built for Lincoln Winters

Lincoln sits out on the Canterbury Plains, and anyone who's lived there knows the inland frosts bite harder than they do closer to the coast. A properly sized heat pump is one of the most cost-effective ways to keep a Lincoln home warm through winter without a nasty power bill, and to take the edge off those hot, still summer afternoons too.

Chill Air has installed heat pumps throughout Lincoln, from the newer estates spreading out around the township to established homes and rural lifestyle blocks on the outskirts. As a certified refrigeration engineer, owner Greg makes sure each system is matched to the property, important in Lincoln, where homes range from compact new builds to large open-plan country houses.
