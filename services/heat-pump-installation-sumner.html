Heat Pump Installation in Sumner

Living by the sea is great, but salt air is hard on heat pumps. We install coastal-spec Daikin, Mitsubishi and Fujitsu systems built to last in Sumner's seaside conditions, sized and sited properly, and backed by a 5-year workmanship warranty.

Heat Pumps for Sumner's Seaside Homes

Sumner is one of Christchurch's most sought-after seaside suburbs, a mix of classic beach baches, character homes and modern builds tucked along the coast and up the hill toward Clifton and Scarborough. It's a beautiful spot to live, but the salt-laden sea air is tough on outdoor equipment, and a standard heat pump that's fine inland can corrode far faster down by the beach.

That's where getting the spec right matters. Chill Air covers the whole of Christchurch, and owner Greg Hicks is a certified refrigeration engineer who'll recommend coastal or marine-grade units with proper corrosion protection, then site the outdoor unit to give it the best shot against the salt air, so your system keeps performing for years.
