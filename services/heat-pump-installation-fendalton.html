Heat Pump Installation in Fendalton

Fendalton's leafy streets are home to some of Christchurch's grandest character houses and larger family properties, and heating them well takes a bit more thought. We install Daikin, Mitsubishi and Fujitsu systems, sized and placed to suit period homes and open-plan living, all backed by a 5-year workmanship warranty.

Heat Pumps for Fendalton's Character Homes & Larger Properties

Fendalton is one of Christchurch's most established suburbs: mature trees, generous sections and a real mix of grand period villas, mid-century homes and substantial modern builds. Heating a home like that well is rarely a one-size-fits-all job. A high-stud villa loses heat differently to a sealed-up new build, and a sprawling open-plan layout needs more than a single unit tucked in a corner.

Chill Air covers the whole of Christchurch, and Fendalton is right in our patch. Owner Greg is a certified refrigeration engineer who will size the system to the home and place it for even, quiet heat and a tidy finish, whether that is a discreet install in a character property or a multi-split to warm several rooms in a larger house.

Discreet Installs for Period & Character Homes

Many Fendalton homes have real character worth protecting, and the last thing you want is hardware that spoils the look. We take care with where units sit, how pipework is run and how the outdoor unit is positioned, so the heating works hard without drawing the eye.

  • Careful placement that respects period features and street appeal
  • Tidy, well-hidden pipe runs and cabling
  • Outdoor units sited sensibly for looks and quiet running
  • Honest advice on what suits an older, high-stud home

Heating Larger, Open-Plan Homes

Bigger Fendalton homes and open-plan living areas often need more than a single high-wall unit to heat evenly. Whether that is a well-placed unit for the main living space, a multi-split across several rooms, or a ducted system for the whole footprint, we will spec it to do the job properly rather than just selling you the biggest box.

  • Free on-site consultation across Fendalton and central Christchurch
  • Quiet, even heating for large living areas and bedrooms
  • Single, multi-split and ducted options sized to the space
  • Summer cooling for those warm Canterbury nor'westers
  • Neat, professional installation with a 5-year workmanship warranty
