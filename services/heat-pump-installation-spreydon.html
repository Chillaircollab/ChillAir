Heat Pumps for Spreydon's Character Homes

Spreydon is one of Christchurch's established southern suburbs, with streets of solid 1920s to 1950s bungalows and character homes sitting alongside newer townhouses and infill. Those older places have plenty of charm, but they can be cold and damp through a Canterbury winter. A well-chosen heat pump is the most efficient way to make them warm and dry without a nasty power bill.

Chill Air is based just down the road in Halswell, so Spreydon is a quick trip for us. Owner Greg Hicks is a certified refrigeration engineer who knows how to position a system in an older home for even, comfortable heat, whether you are replacing a tired log burner or old electric heating, or adding heating and cooling to a renovated open-plan space.
