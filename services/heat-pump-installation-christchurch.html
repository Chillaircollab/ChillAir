Heat Pump Installation in Christchurch

Certified, local heat pump installation across Christchurch, from the central suburbs to the hills, the east, and the growing northwest. The right system for your home, fitted properly and backed by a 5-year workmanship warranty.

Christchurch's Local Heat Pump Specialists

Christchurch homes face everything from frosty, still winter mornings to dry nor'west heat in summer, so a heat pump that's correctly sized and properly installed makes a real difference to both comfort and power bills. Chill Air has installed heat pumps in hundreds of Christchurch properties, from character villas in Riccarton, St Albans and Cashmere to brand-new builds in the northwest and southwest of the city.

As a Christchurch-based, certified refrigeration engineer with over 10 years in the trade, owner Greg Hicks personally makes sure every install is matched to the room, positioned for even heat, and finished tidily. No call centres, no subcontracted crews, just a local team that knows Christchurch homes.

Our Installation Process

We keep it simple and thorough from the first call to the final handover:

  • Free in-home consultation anywhere in Christchurch
  • Honest advice on the right size and model for your space and budget
  • Clean, professional installation by certified technicians
  • Full system testing, commissioning and a clear handover
  • 5-year workmanship warranty and easy after-care

Which Heat Pump Is Right for Your Christchurch Home?

We install and service leading brands including Daikin, Mitsubishi and Fujitsu, across every system type:

  • High wall units: the go-to for living areas and bedrooms
  • Floor consoles: great for older Christchurch homes with limited wall space
  • Multi-split systems: one outdoor unit heating several rooms
  • Ducted systems: discreet, whole-home heating and cooling
