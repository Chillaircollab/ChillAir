Heat Pump Installation in Papanui

From established family homes off Main North Road to the shops and offices around Northlands, the right heat pump keeps the place warm in winter and cool through summer. We install Daikin, Mitsubishi and Fujitsu systems for Papanui homes and businesses, backed by a 5-year workmanship warranty.

Heat Pumps for Papanui Homes & Businesses

Papanui is one of northern Christchurch's busiest hubs, a real mix of established 1960s to 1980s family homes, newer townhouse infill, and the big retail and commercial strip around Northlands and Main North Road. A lot of those older homes still run on electric or fixed heating, which is exactly where a modern heat pump makes a big difference to comfort and the power bill.

Chill Air is a Christchurch-wide business, and we cover the north of the city just as readily as anywhere else. Owner Greg is a certified refrigeration engineer who'll size the system to your room and place it for even, quiet heat, whether it's the lounge of a Papanui family home or the floor of a busy shop.
